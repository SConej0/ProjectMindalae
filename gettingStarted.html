Website

Back to top

Setting up a website consists of getting a domain and hosting server. Putting these two together, gives you a place to create your website and publish it for people to find. The domain will be the name of your website, "business.com or .net, etc.". The hosting server will be where you can build the website.

Hosting Setup

Back to top

A domain is the name of your website, this can be sold separately from the hosting server company you use. Other times, you can buy your domain (a.k.a. website name) through the same server company. A domain can either end in .net, .com, .org or others, depending on what you want. These different endings have different pricings, depending on where you buy them from. Make sure it is one you really like and your customers will be able to find easily or remember.

A hosting service is the site that stores all your data, also known as everything you will put into the website from text to merchandise. A hosting site will literally be hosting you. Think of it as a space you rent for your store. That is why when you combine the domain and hosting site, it is like the domain is your business sign and the hosting site is your store, and now you can start to design the website.

As for pricing, according to WebsiteBuilderExpert, prices can range from $2.49 – $13.95/month for shared hosting and $80 – $506/month for dedicated hosting.

The difference between the two hosting options is, dedicated hosting is more secure than shared hosting since it is not on a shared server like shared hosting is. It is on its own server. From a user’s perspective, loading a website on a dedicated hosting server is faster.

Extra Tips

Back to top

One of the biggest tips I can give from experience is, just do it. Creating a website and dealing with technology can be daunting and can sound like a lot. But if you truly feel like it is time and it is what you want to do. Do it. Take it step by step and enjoy the process. It is going to be hard work but it will also be fun.

Marketing is very important for the success of your business aside from attending festivals. If you do it well and long enough, marketing can increase your sales. It allows your website to be like another booth/store. The key here is to be consistent and get strategic and creative, but above all consistent since it will take time.

Some thing to consider is, really get to know your audience and think about who they are. This will help you relate and cater more to your customers in the design and marketing of your website.

Take photos of products before you publish your website so you can bulk upload them onto the website instead of going back and forth.

An AI copywriting software like CopyAI can help you write your marketing content, but make sure your business' voice is coming through.
