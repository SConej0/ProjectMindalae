So you want to take your business online and need help getting started. We’re here to help! To do so, we’ll share information about what you will need. This consists of knowledge of how to get your website started, tools to create your website and keep organized and finally some marketing tips and resources to get started. Setting up these three things will help you take your business to the next level.   Setting up a website consists of getting a domain and hosting server. Putting these two together, gives you a place to create your website and publish it to the public.

Hosting Setup

Back to top

A domain is the name of your website, this is usually sold separately from the hosting website, through another company. Other times you can buy your domain (a.k.a. Website name) through the same website company. A domain can either end in .net, .com, .org or others, depending on what you want. And these different endings have different pricings, depending on where you buy them from. You are basically purchasing a url, also known as a domain. Make sure it’s one you really like and your customers will be able to find easily or at least remember.   A hosting service is the site that stores all your data, also known as everything you will put into the website from text to merchandise. A hosting site will literally be hosting you. Think of it as a space you rent for your store. That’s why when you put the domain and hosting site together, it’s like the domain is your business sign and the hosting site is your store, and now you can start to design the website.   Prices   According to WebsiteBuilderExpert, prices can range from $2.49 – $13.95/month for shared hosting and $80 – $506/month for dedicated hosting.   The difference between the two hosting options is, dedicated hosting is more secure than shared hosting since it’s not on a shared server like shared hosting is. It’s on its own server. From a user’s perspective, loading a website on a dedicated hosting server is faster.   References: Website Builder Expert: Cost to host a website
